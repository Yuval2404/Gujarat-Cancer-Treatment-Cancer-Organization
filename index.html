About Us

The Gujarat Cancer Treatment Organization (GCTO) is a non-profit dedicated to providing affordable cancer care, awareness, and support for patients across Gujarat.

  • Address: Gujarat University, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India
  • Founded by: Mr. Yuval J Vaghela, Mr. Tushar Bharwad & Mr. Kavya Limbad

Our Mission & Causes

We are committed to defeating cancer through:

  • Funding the treatment of underprivileged patients.
  • Running free cancer screening and early detection camps.
  • Providing counseling and emotional support.
  • Raising awareness about cancer prevention across Gujarat.

Why Cancer Is Dangerous?

  • Cancer can spread fast and affect any organ.
  • High mortality rate without early treatment.
  • Costly treatment strains families emotionally and financially.

Prevention & Precautions

  • Avoid all types of tobacco and chewing gutkha.
  • Eat a balanced, plant-rich diet (fruits & vegetables).
  • Exercise regularly, maintain a healthy weight.
  • Limit alcohol consumption.
  • Use sunscreen, avoid prolonged sun exposure.
  • Go for regular cancer screenings if you're at risk.
  • Practice safe sex and immunize against Hepatitis B and HPV.
